About Me

My name is Nino Kalatozishvili. I studied chemistry for a year at the Agricultural University of Georgia before moving to the United States in May 2025. Now based in Philadelphia, I’m continuing to explore the sciences — especially chemistry — through coursework, independent projects, and growing research interests. I’m particularly interested in the overlap between chemistry and biology, especially in systems that affect everyday life, from environmental processes to human health. Outside of academics, I spend time on photography, music, and dance, which help me stay creative and approach problems from different angles. While my academic path is still developing, I’m motivated by curiosity, depth, and a desire to understand how things work.
